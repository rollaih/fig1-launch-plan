Go-To-Market
Launch Strategy
A 5-part strategy combining video content, influencer seeding, organic distribution, and paid amplification.
01

Commercial-Style Video

Polished commercial showing AI chat responding to a fan, sponsorship section, seamless link experience.

  • Hero Video (60s) — Website, PR, influencer outreach
  • Social Cut (15–30s) — Shareable, vertical, fast hook
  • Demo Walkthrough (90–120s) — Detailed product tour for warm leads
02

Influencer Seeding

The most important lever. Build their pages before outreach — show up with "here's yours, it's live."

  • Hand-pick 5–10 creators across niches
  • Build their pages before outreach
  • Offer first 3–6 months free
  • Send commercial video + live page as the pitch
  • Ask them to post once onboarded
03

Website Product Page

  • Hero section with commercial video
  • Feature breakdown
  • Live interactive demo
  • Social proof & testimonials
  • Comparison vs. competitors
  • Pricing / free trial CTA
04

Fig1.ai Social + PR

  • Launch announcement with video
  • Product deep dive — how AI chat works
  • Creator spotlight series (ongoing)
  • Blog / press release for distribution
05

Paid Promotion (Phase 2)

Activate after organic wave generates social proof.

  • Meta Ads — retargeting + lookalikes from sign-ups
  • Google Ads — "link in bio tool", "linktree alternative"
Execution
Launch Sequence
Phased approach to coordinate all pieces. Adjust timing based on commercial video and creator page readiness.
Pre-Launch
Weeks 1–3
  • Produce commercial video (hero + social cuts)
  • Build pages for 5–10 seeded creators
  • Build website product page with live demo
  • Draft blog / press release
Soft Launch
Week 4
  • Send seeded creators their live pages + video
  • Onboard creators, collect feedback
  • Fig1.ai launch announcement post
  • Publish blog / press release
Public Launch
Weeks 5–6
  • Seeded creators post about their pages
  • Creator spotlight posts (ongoing)
  • Open sign-ups publicly
Growth
Week 7+
  • Activate paid promotion
  • Continue creator outreach
  • Iterate based on early feedback
  • Track "Powered by Fig1.ai" referrals
